About Our Founder & Instructor

Smt. Vaidehi

At Vaidehi Silai Center, we believe that learning a trade is the most direct pathway to self-reliance. Officially registered under the Ministry of MSME, Govt. of India (Udyam Reg No: UDYAM-WB-14-0221168), our center was founded to deliver structured, expert tailoring education to women in West Bengal.

With over 20 years of tailoring craftsmanship, Smt. Vaidehi has mastered fabric architectures, cutting, modern draping, and sewing machine technologies. Her instruction blends practical drafting rules with personal warmth, ensuring that even complete beginners can learn with absolute clarity.

"Every fabric has its own story, and a skilled tailor knows how to drape it. By equipping my students with precise pattern drafting skills, my goal is to give them the freedom to build their own boutique businesses."

Under her guidance, more than 500 students have graduated. Many have successfully established home-based tailoring operations or independent boutique outlets, bringing financial security to their households.

Our Training Philosophy

Precision Drafting

We teach students to draft patterns on grid paper first, preventing fabric wastage and guaranteeing perfect fits.

Hands-On Sewing

Every student receives personalized machines and materials, working closely under Smt. Vaidehi's oversight.

Boutique Model

We train beyond stitching. We offer modules on pricing, fabric sourcing, and local boutique management.
